About Aidan Kennedy

Aidan is a dynamic and accomplished senior at a public high school in Massachusetts. He is deeply involved in both academics and extracurricular activities, demonstrating a strong commitment to personal growth and community involvement. As an athlete, Aidan excels in ski racing and is a dedicated runner, specializing in the mile for his school's track team. His athletic pursuits reflect his dedication to discipline, endurance, and a passion for challenging himself physically. In addition to his athletic achievements, Aidan is a leader in his school community. He serves as the class of 2025 class secretary, where he plays a crucial role in organizing events and representing his peers. His leadership extends to the Model UN team, where he is the head delegate, showcasing his skills in diplomacy, public speaking, and critical thinking. Outside of school, Aidan works as a professional lifeguard and a camp counselor, roles that highlight his responsibility and commitment to the safety and well-being of others. He is also on a path to achieving the rank of Eagle Scout, demonstrating his dedication to service, leadership, and the values of the Scouting movement. Aidan's diverse interests and accomplishments make him a well-rounded and impressive individual, poised for success in his future endeavors.
